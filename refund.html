Refund Policy

At SahilCodeLab, we strive to provide the highest quality digital intelligence. This policy outlines our commitment to transparency regarding payments and refunds for the Wrapify application.

1. Overview

Wrapify requires a one-time payment of ₹99 to unlock premium cinematic analytics. This fee supports our secure, offline-first processing architecture.

2. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are considered under the following specific circumstances:

  • Technical defects that prevent the app from functioning as advertised.
  • Duplicate charges for the same transaction.
  • Refund request made within 7 days of purchase.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Change of mind after a successful digital delivery.
  • Device incompatibility (please verify Android OS support before purchase).
  • Request received after the 7-day window.
  • Suspected abuse of the refund policy.

4. Request Protocol

To request a refund, please email contact@sahilcodelab.com with:

  • Transaction ID (from Razorpay)
  • Payment Date
  • Brief description of the issue

5. Processing Timeline

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment source:

  • UPI: 3-5 Business Days.
  • Cards/NetBanking: 5-7 Business Days.
