Privacy Protocol

Last Modified: March 2026 | Status: Active & Secure

At SahilCodeLab, we believe that your personal conversations should remain just that—personal. Wrapify is engineered with a "Privacy-by-Design" philosophy, ensuring that your data never touches a server, never transits the internet, and never leaves your control.

1. Local Neural Processing

Wrapify performs all chat analysis locally on your smartphone. We do not use cloud-based parsing or external processing APIs. The raw data from your exported WhatsApp chats is processed entirely within the application's secure sandbox using your device's local memory (RAM) and processor.

2. No Data Collection

Our commitment to privacy is simple: we collect nothing.

  • No User Profiles: We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • No Tracking: We don't use 3rd-party analytics like Google Analytics or Firebase.
  • No Cloud Sync: There is no "backup to cloud" feature. Your insights live and die on your device.

3. Input Data Integrity

When you import a .txt chat export:

  • The file is temporarily read into memory.
  • It is parsed into statistical tokens (message counts, emoji counts, timing data).
  • The raw text is NOT stored permanently by the app unless you explicitly save a generated visual card to your gallery.

4. Permission Logic

Wrapify only requests the minimum permissions required to function:

  • Storage Access: Required to read the exported chat file you select and to save your generated Wrapped cards.
  • No Internet Permission: The app is designed to work 100% offline.

5. Contact SahilCodeLab

If you have questions about our security architecture or want to verify the cryptographic integrity of the app, please contact us through SahilCodeLab's official channels. We are committed to transparency and user sovereignty.
