Your Data.

Wrapify is engineered by SahilCodeLab with absolute privacy at its core. We don't believe in cloud storage for your personal memories.

01. Zero Cloud Policy

Every calculation, from message frequency to relationship mapping, happens on your phone's processor. No remote servers are involved in the parsing of your conversations.

02. No Data Harvest

We do not collect usage statistics, analytics, or behavioral data. Our business model is built on trust, not your identity.

03. Sandbox Processing

  • Encrypted Reading: Files are read into a temporary memory sandbox.
  • Instant Wipe: All raw text data is cleared from memory once the visualization is generated.
  • User Sovereignty: You decide what to save, what to share, and what to keep private.

04. Contact

If you wish to audit our protocol or have security inquiries, reach out to the developer at SahilCodeLab.
