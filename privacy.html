Security Protocol

At SahilCodeLab, we believe that your personal memories belong to you. Wrapify is engineered with a strict local-first philosophy.

01. Absolute Localism

Your WhatsApp chat history is a private dialogue. Wrapify parses this data entirely on your device's processor. No cloud APIs, no external synchronization, and no remote databases.

02. Zero Collection Policy

Our commitment to privacy is simple: we do not collect what we do not need.

  • No User Identification: We do not require account creation, phone numbers, or emails.
  • No Tracking: We use zero 3rd-party analytics. No Google Firebase, no Meta Pixel.
  • No Behavioral Harvesting: Your interactions within the app are not monitored.

03. Input Cryptography

When you import a .txt export, the data enters a temporary memory sandbox. Once the visual insights are rendered and you exit the screen, the raw text is immediately wiped from the system RAM.

04. Hardware Privacy

Wrapify only requests access to local storage to read your selected file and save your generated masterpieces. We operate flawlessly in **Airplane Mode**, proving our 100% offline commitment.
