Security Protocol

Privacy Policy

At SahilCodeLab, your privacy is not a feature; it is our foundation. Wrapify is engineered with a strict local-first philosophy.

1. Absolute Localism

Your WhatsApp chat history is a private dialogue. Wrapify parses this data entirely on your device's processor. No cloud APIs, no external synchronization, and no remote databases. We literally cannot see your data even if we wanted to.

2. Zero Collection Policy

  • No User Identification: No accounts, phone numbers, or emails required.
  • No Tracking: Zero 3rd-party analytics. No Firebase, no Meta Pixel.
  • No Harvesting: Your interactions are never monitored or recorded.

3. Input Integrity

When you import a .txt export, the data enters a temporary memory sandbox. Once the visual insights are rendered and you exit the screen, the raw text is immediately purged from the system RAM.

4. Hardware Transparency

Wrapify only requests storage permissions to read your selected file and save generated insights. The app operates flawlessly in **Airplane Mode**, guaranteeing its offline nature.

5. Contact

Innovated by Sahil in India, Wrapify is a tribute to the sanctity of the individual. For security inquiries, contact contact@sahilcodelab.com.
